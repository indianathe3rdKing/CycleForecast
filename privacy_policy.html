CycleForecast ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This application is designed to function without collecting personally identifiable user information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

CycleForecast does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or account details.

2. Permissions and Data Usage

a. Location Access

CycleForecast may request access to your device location to provide accurate, location-based weather forecasts and cycling recommendations. Location data is used only in real-time and is not stored or shared.

b. Notifications

The app may send notifications such as daily weather alerts and cycling recommendations. These notifications can be enabled or disabled at any time in your device or app settings.

c. Device and Battery Optimization

CycleForecast may request permission to manage battery optimization settings to ensure reliable background updates for notifications.

3. Local Data Storage

CycleForecast uses local storage (such as Android DataStore) to save user preferences, including:

  • Selected units (metric/imperial)
  • Notification settings
  • Saved locations (if applicable)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted externally.

4. Third-Party Services

CycleForecast uses third-party services to function, including:

  • Weather APIs (such as Open-Meteo) to retrieve weather data
  • Networking libraries (e.g., Retrofit, OkHttp) to communicate with external services

These services may process basic request data (such as location coordinates) necessary to deliver weather information.

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

Since CycleForecast does not store personal user data on external servers, risks associated with data storage are minimized. However, we implement reasonable security practices to ensure safe operation.

6. User Control

  • Disable location access at any time via device settings
  • Turn off notifications in app or system settings
  • Clear app data directly from their device
